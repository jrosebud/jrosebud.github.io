    Assignment 2: Logo and Style Guide

    You will design a logo for your company, and an accompanying style guide. Assignment is due Wednesday, September 25 by 9 a.m.. Specs for the assignment are in this PDF. You must turn in digital files for your logo, as well as your logo printed and centered on 8.5 x 11 paper (cardstock is preferred). You will also need to turn in a style guide and a document with 1-2 paragraphs explaining your design choices and why you designed what you did.

    If you need to look at a sample style guide, you may find one here.

    Turn in your digital files to Dropbox here. You must turn in a .zip file (so put all your files in a folder and then zip it up). You will turn in your physical copies in class.

    PLEASE DO NOT FORGET TO PROPERLY PACKAGE YOUR DIGITAL FILES AND MAKE SURE ALL YOUR ASSETS TRAVEL WITH YOUR MAIN FILE!
