    You will design a final promotional package for your record label or podcast. You must design the packaging for this promotional item, and all its contents. Please read the specs for this final carefully! You can include any other promotional items in your project that you wish (for example, t-shirts, postcards, keychains, trinkets, etc.). Assignment is due Thursday, May 1 at noon. Specs for the assignment are in this PDF. You must turn in all the digital files associated with your promotional package. Any physical components must be brought to class the day the final is due.

    Turn in your digital files to this Dropbox link. You must turn in your entire package for your item, fonts and file links and everything.
