    Assignment 2: Logo & Business Card

    You will design a logo and a two-sided business card for your record company. Assignment is due Monday, September 21 by noon.. Specs for the assignment are here. You must turn in digital files of your business card, as well as your logo centered on an 8.5 x 11-inch canvas.

    Turn in your digital files to Dropbox here. You must turn in a .zip file (so put all your files in a folder and then zip it up). You will turn in your physical copies in class.

    PLEASE DO NOT FORGET TO PROPERLY PACKAGE YOUR DIGITAL FILES AND MAKE SURE ALL YOUR ASSETS TRAVEL WITH YOUR MAIN FILE!
