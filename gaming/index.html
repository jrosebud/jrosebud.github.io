    • JOUR 490 - Narrative Gaming

    You're probably here because you're looking for information about JOUR 490: Narrative Gaming, a directed research class being taught by Profs. Laura Davis and Jenn de la Fuente. It will be offered in Fall 2018.

    Here are some game examples being used in journalism. The purpose of this class is to build something similar. You'll report on your idea, think of how to gamify it, and make it come alive! We'll also learn how game concepts can help engage people in news!

    Examples

    • The Voter Suppression Trail - New York Times
    • Can you get around town as a wheelchair user? - BuzzFeed
    • Play Fake News!
    • PolitiTruth: a Politifact Fake News Game
    • The Uber Game: Can you make it in the gig economy? - Financial Times
    • The classic: Oregon Trail (yes, you can play it at this link)
